Account Deletion - Profigram

At Profigram, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can delete your account directly from the mobile application at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Profigram app.
  2. Go to your Profile.
  3. Scroll down to the very bottom of the screen. You will see a red button with text Delete Account and a delete icon.
  4. Tap on it and confirm deletion in the dialog that appears.

What Happens After Deletion

  • All personal data (phone number, name, profile information, chat history, and orders) will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Some data required for legal, regulatory, or security reasons may be retained for up to 90 days before being automatically deleted.
